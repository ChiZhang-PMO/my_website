About

I study how different dark matter models (CDM, SIDM, velocity‑dependent SIDM, WDM) shape gas and neutral hydrogen in and around halos, and how these leave signatures in Lyman‑α spectra and galaxy–IGM cross‑correlations. I work with high‑performance computing on Leonardo and analyze large simulation datasets.

Research Highlights

Gas & HI profiles in SIDM

Systematic comparison of gas/HI radial profiles across DM models; core formation and ionization balance.
