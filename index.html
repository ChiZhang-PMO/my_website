I grow up under the clear sky with mild wind from grass land, build my free characteristic. The mystery dark night sky in my hometown attracts me a lot and inspiring my interest of ASTRONOMY. Therefore I become a Ph.D. student in astrophysics at PMO and USTC after I finish my undergraduate in Physics at Inner-Mongolia University.
